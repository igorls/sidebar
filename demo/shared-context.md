## Definitions (so the panels mean the same thing to everyone)

- **Gross churn** — % of MRR lost to cancellations + downgrades this month.
- **NRR** — net revenue retention incl. expansion (>100% = expansion beats churn).
- **WAU** — accounts with ≥1 active session in the last 7 days ÷ active accounts.
- **"Who's still with us"** — retention by signup cohort (the curve we never have on hand).

## Segments

- **SMB** — ~70% of logos, the churn problem (cancels on price + low activation).
- **Mid-Market** — ~25% of logos, sticky, NRR > 110%, where expansion comes from.
- **Enterprise** — small, new this quarter, 3 logos in onboarding.

## Top churn reasons (last 90 days, cancellation survey)
